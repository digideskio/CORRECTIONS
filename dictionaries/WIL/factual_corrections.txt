It is required by rules of Sanskrit grammar to have to have cC for
non-dhAtus:
छे च rule gives this. ह्रस्व+छ => ह्रस्व+त्‌+छ.

विछ -> वित्छ (by छे च) -> विच्छ (by स्तोः श्चुना श्चुः).

So the root in धातुपाठ is विछ. In usage it is विच्छ
